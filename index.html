The Intersection

Innovation in pharma rarely fails because the science was unsound.

It fails in the space between disciplines.

Science optimizes for truth. Regulation optimizes for risk control. Business optimizes for capital efficiency and time.

When those systems are not aligned from day one, failure becomes structural — and structural failures compound quietly.

"In regulated markets, value is created — or destroyed — at the intersection."
